Privacy Policy

Overview

This application does not collect, store, or process any personal data from users. We do not require accounts, registration, or any form of payment.

Advertising

The app displays advertisements provided by third-party ad networks. These ad networks may collect certain data to show relevant ads. This data collection is handled entirely by the ad networks and is subject to their own privacy policies.

Ad networks may collect:

  • Device information (device type, operating system)
  • Ad interaction data
  • IP address
  • Advertising identifiers (such as Google Advertising ID or IDFA)

We do not:

  • Collect or store any of this data ourselves
  • Track your activity
  • Create user profiles
  • Share your data with anyone (because we don't collect it)

Children's Privacy
